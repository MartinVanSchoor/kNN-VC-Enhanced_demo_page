kNN-VC-Enhanced - Training-free, any-to-any one-shot voice conversion

Marthinus Enslin Van Schoor

[code]

I introduce kNN-VC-Enhanced, an improvement of the kNN-VC model, which features improved performance for low target data scenarios such as one-shot voice conversion, and provides a means for efficient, training-free conversion which works for low resource (limited computing power) scenarios. This work provides a model which is robust and practical for a wide variety of applications, as conversions can be made using only a single, short utterance from the target speaker. The goal of this demo pages is to provide the reader of the report with real examples to consult as they read relevant sections of the report, to gain a better understanding of choices made and how the results actually sound, rather than just looking at graphs and tables.
